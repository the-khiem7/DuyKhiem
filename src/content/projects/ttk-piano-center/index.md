## Overview

TTK Piano Center is a Java web application for managing courses, enrollments, cart flow, and administrative operations. It reflects earlier full-stack work where the emphasis was on getting the business process online with dependable CRUD and workflow behavior.

## Functional Areas

- Course catalog and enrollment
- Cart and checkout-like interaction
- Admin operations
- Database-backed business flow

## Why Keep It In The Portfolio

Even though it is not as architecture-heavy as my later work, it helps show progression from traditional web application development toward more workflow-heavy backend systems.
