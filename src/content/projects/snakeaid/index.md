## Overview

SnakeAid is a practical healthcare product built around one high-pressure problem: helping users respond quickly and correctly during snakebite incidents. The product combines first-aid guidance, rescue coordination, snake identification, and expert support into a single workflow.

## What I Focused On

I treated this project as more than a demo AI app. The goal was to make the backend and product flow support decision-making during stressful moments, where users need clear next actions rather than complex UI.

## System Thinking

- Designed the experience around response flow instead of isolated features.
- Balanced AI-assisted identification with operational steps such as escalation and coordination.
- Framed the product as a reliability problem, not just a model integration problem.

## Why It Matters

This project reflects the kind of work I enjoy most: systems where backend structure, trust, and product clarity all matter at the same time.
