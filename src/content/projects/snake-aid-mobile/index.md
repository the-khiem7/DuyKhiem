## Overview

Snake Aid Mobile is the mobile companion for the SnakeAid platform. It brings emergency response flow closer to end users by supporting SOS signaling, rescue tracking, and AI-assisted snake identification in a mobile-first experience.

## Why It Matters

This project complements the backend and AI layers of SnakeAid by showing how the platform extends into field-facing workflows.
