## Overview

Zima Linux Homelab is a personal self-hosted environment used to explore containers, routing, reverse proxies, tunnels, and service operations in a hands-on setting.

## What It Covers

- Running and managing self-hosted services
- Reverse proxy and network access patterns
- Container-oriented operations
- Practical experimentation outside classroom or internship constraints

## Why It Matters

This kind of project sharpens the operational side of my backend skill set by forcing me to think about deployability, exposure, reliability, and maintainability in a real environment.
